Welcome

Ninesenses is an independent and collaborative creative consultancy based in the centre of Lancaster, England. Established in 2016 by multi-disciplinary designer and developer Tom Skarbek-Wazynski.

About

Hi, I’m Tom. With over 9 years experience, I work with individuals, start-ups and established businesses planning, designing and building bespoke digital products and brands.

Services

I specialise in brand identity, creative direction, design and bespoke development. With a particular focus on always delivering high-quality solutions, through creative integrity, research, performance and maintainability.
